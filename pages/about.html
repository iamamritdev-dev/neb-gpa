About NEB GPA Calculator

Empowering Nepal's students with accurate, free, and privacy-first academic tools since 2024.

Who We Are

NEB GPA Calculator is a free academic tool built specifically for students studying under the Nepal Education Board (NEB) grading system. We cover SEE (Class 10), Grade 11, and Grade 12 across Science, Management, Humanities, and Education streams.

The project was founded with one simple goal: give every Nepali student instant, accurate, and completely private access to their academic standing — without needing to sign up, pay, or share personal data.

Our Mission

To eliminate confusion around the NEB grading system by providing clear, accurate, and easy-to-use tools that help students plan their academic future with confidence.

Whether you're calculating your Science stream GPA, checking grades for Management, or reviewing your SEE result, our tools are designed to give you reliable answers in seconds.

Our Values

Accuracy First

Calculations strictly follow official NEB grading policies and credit-hour weightings.

Privacy by Default

All calculations run in your browser. No data is ever collected or stored.

Always Free

Every tool on this platform is 100% free — no ads, no premium tiers.

Mobile Ready

Optimized for phones, tablets, and desktops with zero friction.

Grading System We Follow

All calculations are based on the official NEB grading system which uses a 4.0 GPA scale. The grade scale ranges from A+ (Outstanding, 4.0) down to NG (Not Graded, 0.0). Our tool computes weighted GPAs using each subject's theory and practical credit-hour split as mandated by NEB.

Read more in our blog or visit the grading system section on the homepage.
