Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to NEB GPA Calculator. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website.

2. Client-Side Processing (Zero Data Collection)

Our GPA calculators operate entirely on the client-side (in your web browser). We do not collect, store, transmit, or save any of the academic data, marks, or grades you input into our calculators. Once you close your browser tab or reload the page, all entered data is immediately erased.

3. Information We Do Collect

When you use the website, we may collect anonymous, non-personally identifiable information through standard analytics tools (like Google Analytics). This includes:

  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • General location (country/city level)

This information helps us understand how our tools are used and how we can improve them.

4. Contact Forms

If you choose to contact us via our contact form, we will collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and do not share it with third parties or use it for marketing purposes without your consent.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure the basic functionality of the website and analytics cookies to understand site traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some minor features might be affected.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact Page.
