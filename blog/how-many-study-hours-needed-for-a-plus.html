How Many Study Hours Are Needed to Get A+ in Class 12 (NEB, Nepal)?

June 4, 2026 Amrit KC 5 min read

Getting an A+ (GPA 4.0) in Class 12 under the NEB system is a goal many students aim for. But one of the most common questions is: "How many hours should I study daily?"

The answer is not just about hours — it’s about quality, consistency, and smart strategy.

Understanding A+ in NEB

In Nepal’s NEB grading system, an A+ requires 90–100 marks (GPA 4.0). This means you must perform excellent in every subject, not just one.

Ideal Study Hours for A+

Daily Study Target:

  • 4 hours of focused self-study (minimum)
  • Excluding school/coaching time

For Top Students (A+ Aim):

  • 4-6 hours daily (during serious preparation phase)
  • Can go up to 8–10 hours near exams (with proper breaks)

Important: Studying 10 hours inefficiently is worse than 5 hours focused study.

Smart Study vs Hard Study

Many students fail not because they study less, but because they study wrong.

Wrong Way:

  • Reading without understanding
  • Memorizing without concept
  • No revision

Right Way:

  • Concept-based learning
  • Solving past papers
  • Active recall + revision

Best Daily Study Routine (Example)

Morning (2–3 hrs)

  • Study difficult subjects (Physics, Math, Account)
  • Brain is fresh → better understanding

Afternoon (1–2 hrs) if holiday

  • Practice numericals

Evening/Night (1.5+hrs)

  • Revision + past questions

Key Strategy to Get A+

1. Follow NEB Textbook Strictly

Most exam questions come from textbook concepts.

2. Practice Past Papers

  • Solve 5–7 years questions
  • Understand patterns

3. Focus on Practicals

  • Practicals carry marks (often 25%)
  • Easy way to boost GPA

4. Consistency > Motivation

Studying daily 5–6 hours is better than studying 12 hours once a week.

Biggest Mistake Students Make

“I will study seriously only before exam.”

This is dangerous.

A+ students:

  • Start early
  • Revise multiple times
  • Build strong basics

Final Truth (Very Important)

There is no fixed number of hours for A+
But there is a minimum standard of consistency

Minimum:

  • 3-4 hours daily (consistent)

Ideal for A+:

  • 4–8 hours smart study
  • Strong revision + practice

Conclusion

To achieve an A+ in Class 12 NEB:

  • Focus on quality over quantity
  • Study consistently every day
  • Practice and revise regularly
